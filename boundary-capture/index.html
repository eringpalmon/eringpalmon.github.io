How to Play
The Cross Target
Each round spawns a glowing ✛ cross. Your goal is to trap it inside a polygon by connecting dots. All 4 arm tips must be inside your shape!
Tap or Drag
Tap individual dots to add them. Or drag your finger across the grid — dots you pass through get selected automatically!
Auto-Capture!
No button to press — the moment all 4 cross tips are enclosed, it triggers automatically. The shape turns green and you score!
Score Big
Fewer dots = more points. A 3-dot triangle scores up to 500 pts. Speed bonus + area bonus stack on top!
Watch Out!
Don't let the timer bar run out. Avoid red obstacles that spawn over time. Either one ends your game!
